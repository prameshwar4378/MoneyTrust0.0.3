What is a Personal Loan?

Features of Personal Loan

What makes Personal Loan a Perfect Choice!


No Collateral or Security

No collateral or mortgage to avail a personal loan. Loan approval is based on your credit score, credit report, repayment history, and other criteria.

End-Usage-Flexible

No end-usage restrictions on a Personal Loan gives you the liberty to meet multiple purposes including medical emergencies, holidays, business, or debt consolidation.

Flexible Tenure

You may avail a loan for a flexible period ranging from 12 months and 72 months.

Minimal Documentation

Identity proof, proof of residence or address, and income proof are the only requirement for a personal loan.

Quick Disbursal

Once approved, disbursal of your personal loan happens within a few hours. If you avail a pre-approved personal loan, the disbursal would be as short as a few minutes.

Flexible Loan Amount

Factors determining your loan value are repayment history, monthly income, age, profession, employer reputation, etc.

Overview

An unsecured borrowing, the term Personal Loan implies an amount of money borrowed for a variety of purposes. This multi-purpose loan has no end-usage restrictions, and can be used to consolidate debt, pay for home renovation, organize a wedding, or any other purpose the borrower deems fit. The borrowing has to be repaid over a period with interest along with other fees and charges.

Personal Loans are collateral-free unsecured loans offered by almost all Banks and NBFCs, and are approved within 24 hours to 7 business days. These funds are multi-purpose loans without end-usage restrictions, and thus can be used for varied purposes ranging from emergencies to vacations. Easy documentation and quick approval makes these collateral-free loans easy to avail and hassle-free in times of need.

EMI Calculator

The term Equated Monthly Income or EMI refers to a fixed amount of money paid by a borrower to a lender at a pre-agreed date every month. An EMI includes both the interest and the principal amount payable each month over a specified period until you pay off your Personal Loan completely. Other than Personal Loans, Home Loans, Business Loans, Loans Against Property, Car and Auto Loans, and some other types of loans accept this method of loan repayment.
